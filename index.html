About Us

ASE was started in 2016 , by a small motley crowd of Science & Technology Entrepreneur Parks and Technology Business Entrepreneurs that were certified under the umbrella of the Department of Industrial Promotions and Policies (DIPP) , Government of India. Today it has grown to a membership base of 300+ organisations that are into entrepreneurship development and incubation.


Its main objective is “To promote business incubation support activities in the country through exchangte of information, sharing of experience, and other networking assistance among Startup Companies approved by Department of Industrial Promotions and Policies (DIPP)".
